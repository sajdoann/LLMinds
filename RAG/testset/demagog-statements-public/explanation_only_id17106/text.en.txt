<p>Since January 2018, pensions have actually <a href="https://www.cssz.cz/cz/o-cssz/informace/media/tiskove-zpravy/tiskove-zpravy-2017/2017-12-06-od-ledna-se-valorizuji-vsechny-duchody-cssz-provede-jejich-zvyseni-automaticky.htm">been valorized</a> . The amount of CZK 475 represents an increase in the average pension, which in September 2017 was CZK 11,828. Therefore, it may be higher or lower for some pension recipients.</p><p> This valorization was approved in September 2017 by the government of Bohuslav Sobotka, i.e. the coalition government.</p><p> The government of Andrej Babiš, which ruled without confidence from December 13, 2017 to June 27, 2018 (i.e. 196 days, which we assess as an insignificant deviation; we leave the assessment of the quality of the government without confidence to the reader) <a href="https://www.vlada.cz/cz/media-centrum/aktualne/kabinet-andreje-babise-schvalil-navyseni-duchodu-v-prumeru-o-918-korun-mesicne-163624/">approved</a> the pension insurance amendment in February 2018.</p><p> According to this proposal, pensioners will actually receive an average <a href="https://ct24.ceskatelevize.cz/ekonomika/2403196-zmeny-v-duchodech-prosly-vladou-budou-stat-kolem-14-miliard-korun">increase</a> of CZK 918 (CZK 540 each plus the rest according to the merit part of the pension) from January 2019. The increase in pensions will be an average of CZK 598,&quot; informed the outgoing head of the state treasury, Alena Schillerová (for ANO).<br/><br/> Pensioners over 85 years of age receive an additional CZK 1,000.</p><p> The deputies <a href="https://ct24.ceskatelevize.cz/domaci/2572831-zive-poslanci-nejspis-podpori-vladni-verzi-zvysovani-duchodu">agreed</a> to approve the change in pension amounts in August 2018, but the president&#39;s signature is still awaited.</p>
