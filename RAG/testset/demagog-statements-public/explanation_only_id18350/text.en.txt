<p>First of all, let us state that the part of the statement where Petr Fiala states that it was not easy for ODS not to field its candidate in every district is an opinion statement, and therefore we will not evaluate it as such.</p><p> ODS can be considered a traditional political party on the Czech scene (it was <a href="https://aplikace.mvcr.cz/seznam-politickych-stran/Vypis_Rejstrik.aspx?id=120">founded</a> in 1991). The part of the statement regarding the 2018 Senate elections can also be considered completely true. New senators were elected in <a href="https://www.volby.cz/pls/senat/se1111?xjazyk=CZ&amp;xdatum=20181005&amp;xv=7&amp;xt=1">27</a> districts and ODS nominated its representative in only<a href="https://www.volby.cz/pls/senat/se1213?xjazyk=CZ&amp;xdatum=20181005&amp;xobvod=0&amp;xv=1">17</a> of them.</p><p> In other districts, ODS nominated a coalition candidate together with other parties. Such candidates included <a href="https://www.volby.cz/pls/senat/se2111?XJAZYK=CZ&amp;XDATUM=20181005&amp;xobvod=44">Jan Tecl</a> , who was nominated by ODS together with STAN, <a href="https://www.volby.cz/pls/senat/se2111?XJAZYK=CZ&amp;XDATUM=20181005&amp;xobvod=68">Pavel Herbert</a> , a joint candidate of TOP 09, ODS and STAN, and <a href="https://www.volby.cz/pls/senat/se2111?XJAZYK=CZ&amp;XDATUM=20181005&amp;xobvod=59">Mikuláš Bek</a> .</p><p> Furthermore, ODS <a href="https://www.irozhlas.cz/zpravy-domov/ods-volby-senat-obcanska-demokraticka-strana-cervicek-bek-fischer-kratina_1805261709_haf">supported,</a> for example, the candidacies of Pavel Fischer in Prague, Hana Žáková in Třebíč, and Lukáš Libor in Zlín. It <a href="https://www.volby.cz/pls/senat/se2111?XJAZYK=CZ&amp;XDATUM=20181005&amp;xobvod=2">did not field</a> or <a href="https://www.irozhlas.cz/zpravy-domov/ods-volby-senat-obcanska-demokraticka-strana-cervicek-bek-fischer-kratina_1805261709_haf">support</a> any candidate in the Sokolov district.</p><p> At the same time, ODS clearly <a href="https://www.volby.cz/pls/senat/se1111?xjazyk=CZ&amp;xdatum=20181005&amp;xv=7&amp;xt=1">dominated</a> the elections, with its candidates winning <a href="https://www.volby.cz/pls/senat/se1213?xjazyk=CZ&amp;xdatum=20181005&amp;xv=2">a total of</a> 9 seats ahead of the second-place STAN movement with 3 mandates.</p>
