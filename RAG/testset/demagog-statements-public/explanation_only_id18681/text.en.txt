<p>Over the past few months, the opposition has clashed with supporters <a href="http://www.psp.cz/sqw/historie.sqw?t=509&amp;o=8">of the government&#39;s tax package</a> in the Chamber of Deputies. For example, Miroslav Kalousek from TOP 09 described the new taxation of <a href="https://www.ceskenoviny.cz/zpravy/opozice-opet-kritizovala-danovy-balicek-ministryne-ho-hajila/1801587">insurance company reserves</a> as the most dangerous and harmful part of the package and proposed <a href="http://www.psp.cz/sqw/text/orig2.sqw?idd=162339">to cancel</a> this change (.doc, p. 2). Miroslav Kalousek further claims that the tightening of conditions will motivate foreign owners of insurance companies in the Czech Republic to send dividends abroad.</p><p> Another criticized point of the law is the increase in the fee for <a href="https://ct24.ceskatelevize.cz/domaci/2952944-zive-snemovna-mela-schvalovat-vladni-danovy-balicek">registering a property in the cadastre</a> from the current 1,000 crowns to double it. MPs Věra Kovářová (STAN), Mikuláš Ferjenčík (Pirates) and Jan Bartošek (KDU-ČSL) <a href="https://www.ceskenoviny.cz/zpravy/opozice-opet-kritizovala-danovy-balicek-ministryne-ho-hajila/1801587">submitted</a> a joint <a href="http://www.psp.cz/sqw/text/orig2.sqw?idd=162386">proposal</a> (.doc) in which they want to delete this part from the proposal.</p><p> The last of the opposition proposals mentioned in the statement is the attempt to abolish <a href="https://www.ceskenoviny.cz/zpravy/opozice-opet-kritizovala-danovy-balicek-ministryne-ho-hajila/1801587">the real estate acquisition tax</a> , which is being pursued <a href="http://www.psp.cz/sqw/text/orig2.sqw?idd=162092">by the ODS</a> (.doc). <a href="https://www.lidovky.cz/domov/dan-z-nabyti-nemovitosti-se-bude-platit-dal-snemovna-odmitla-navrh-senatu-na-zruseni.A190924_203904_ln_domov_ele">The Senate</a> attempted the same change, but it was also rejected. However, none of these proposals <a href="http://www.psp.cz/sqw/text/orig2.sqw?idd=168103">were</a> (.doc, pp. 4–5, 9) adopted.</p><p> On the contrary, the Pirates&#39; amendments to improve conditions for <a href="http://www.psp.cz/sqw/text/orig2.sqw?idd=162409">home brewing</a> and <a href="http://www.psp.cz/sqw/text/orig2.sqw?idd=161936">poker</a> players were <a href="http://www.psp.cz/sqw/text/orig2.sqw?idd=168103">accepted</a> in the Chamber of Deputies (.doc, pp. 7, 9).</p><p></p>
