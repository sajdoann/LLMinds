<p>Pirate Party National <a href="https://wiki.pirati.cz/volby/start">Election Team</a><a href="https://wiki.pirati.cz/volby/start"></a> consists of an election committee and an election coordinator. The functioning of the election committee is regulated by <a href="https://wiki.pirati.cz/rules/vr#volebni_kampan">Section 15c</a> of the Election Code. The election committee currently has 6 people and its chairman is Senator <a href="https://www.pirati.cz/lide/lukas-wagenknecht/">Lukáš Wagenknecht</a> . He was unanimously elected to the position by the members of the election committee at an introductory <a href="https://forum.pirati.cz/viewtopic.php?f=858&amp;t=35968&amp;start=10#p637788">meeting</a> held on July 19, 2019.</p><p> Other members include <a href="https://www.pirati.cz/lide/karolina-sadilkova/">Karolína Sadílková</a> (representative of the media department), <a href="https://wiki.pirati.cz/lide/helena_vondrakova">Helena Vondráková</a> (coordinator of the Liberec region and representative of the personnel department), <a href="https://www.pirati.cz/lide/petr-fojtik/">Petr Fojtík</a> , <a href="https://wiki.pirati.cz/lide/kamil_kubica">Kamil Kubica</a> and <a href="https://cz.linkedin.com/in/ales-krupa-89b23933">Aleš Krupa</a> .
