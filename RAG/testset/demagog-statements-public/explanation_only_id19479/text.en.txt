<p>Petr Vokřál is <a href="https://www.seznamzpravy.cz/clanek/mistopredseda-ano-vokral-ve-strane-konci-108201">a former</a> vice-chairman of the ANO movement and the Brno <a href="https://zpravy.aktualne.cz/domaci/jsem-pracujici-nezamestnany-tvrdi-mistopredseda-ano-vokral/r~bd03be58a7c211e9a01c0cc47ab5f122/">expresario</a> . He left the movement at the beginning of June this year <a href="https://www.seznamzpravy.cz/clanek/mistopredseda-ano-vokral-ve-strane-konci-108201">because</a> : &quot; <i>he no longer wants to associate his name with people who use the ANO movement only for their personal benefit (…)</i> &quot;. He is referring to the Stoka corruption <a href="https://zpravy.aktualne.cz/domaci/vokral-zrusit-cele-brnenske-ano-jsem-navrhoval-uz-davno/r~c2e44a22acb211ea95caac1f6b220ee8/">case</a> , which involved <a href="https://www.irozhlas.cz/zpravy-domov/stoka-brno-stred-korupce-kauza-ano-svachula-manipulace_2007081456_ban">bribes</a> and influencing contracts worth up to 130 million crowns.</p><p> In <a href="https://zpravy.aktualne.cz/domaci/vokral-zrusit-cele-brnenske-ano-jsem-navrhoval-uz-davno/r~c2e44a22acb211ea95caac1f6b220ee8/">an interview</a> with Aktuálně.cz, Petr Vokřál said that after the decision to abolish three party cells in Židenice, Starý Lískovec and Černovice, which he proposed, was revoked by the national presidency, he proposed <i>&quot;that the whole of Brno be abolished and rebuilt&quot;</i> again. Originally, <a href="https://www.ceskenoviny.cz/zpravy/vedeni-ano-pozastavilo-clenstvi-faltynka-mladsiho-v-hnuti/1889654">the decision</a> to abolish the three cells in question was made in March 2020. However, it was subsequently attacked by some members of the abolished cells as <a href="https://ekonomickydenik.cz/clen-zrusene-bunky-ano-brna-se-kvuli-zrusenemu-clenstvi-obraci-rozhodci-komisi-vedeni-hnuti-se-chova-hur-nez-komunisti-tvrdi/">illegitimate</a> .</p><p> On May 14, the nationwide ANO presidium expressed its opinion on the issue at its meeting. The presidium granted the members&#39; request and annulled the dissolution of the three Brno cells due to an alleged procedural error. At the same meeting, the ANO presidium also rejected another proposal by Vokřál to <a href="https://www.idnes.cz/brno/zpravy/petr-vokral-ano-brno-exprimator-neshody-vedeni.A200515_547609_brno-zpravy_mls">dissolve</a> the entire Brno organization.</p><p> A <a href="https://www.ceskenoviny.cz/zpravy/vedeni-ano-prijalo-navrh-na-zruseni-brnenske-organizace/1902947">month</a> later, at <a href="https://zpravy.aktualne.cz/domaci/politika/babis-navrhne-predsednictvu-ano-organizaci-v-brne-zrusit-a-z/r~d24e7254ac1611ea8972ac1f6b220ee8/">the suggestion</a> of Andrej Babiš, the decision <i>to &quot;cancele the whole of Brno&quot;</i> and re-create the Brno branch was approved (which <a href="https://www.irozhlas.cz/zpravy-domov/brno-ano-tatana-mala-andrej-babis-lubomir-wenzl-jihomoravsky-kraj_2007021747_sto">happened</a> after Petr Vokřál left the movement).</p>
