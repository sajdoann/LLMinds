<p>Despite all the measures taken, <a href="https://www.reuters.com/article/uk-health-coronavirus-spain-factbox/timeline-how-the-coronavirus-spread-in-spain-idINKBN21K0L7">Spain</a> and <a href="https://www.reuters.com/article/us-health-coronavirus-italy-idUSKBN21D327">Italy</a> were unable to slow down the coronavirus epidemic for a long time in the spring of this year. In March, the number of deaths in Spain even <a href="https://ct24.ceskatelevize.cz/svet/3066876-cina-hlasi-pokles-novych-pripadu-koronaviru-prvniho-nakazeneho-ma-libye">exceeded</a> the number of victims in China at that time. Subsequently, both countries <a href="https://www.natoaktual.cz/zpravy/spanelsko-nato-koronavirus-humanitarni-pomoc-italie-cerna-hora-aliance.A200327_123430_na_zpravy_m00">asked</a> for help from their NATO allies through <a href="https://www.mvcr.cz/clanek/euroatlanticke-koordinacni-stredisko-pro-reseni-situace-pri-katastrofach.aspx">the Euro</a> -Atlantic Disaster Response Coordination Centre.</p><p> This center is now coordinating shipments of medical supplies, such as respirators, masks and ventilators, which are urgently needed by allied countries. In response to this request, the Czech Republic <a href="https://www.mzv.cz/madrid/cz/bilateralni_vztahy/cesko_nabidne_spanelsku_10_000.html">donated</a> 10,000 protective suits to Spain and Italy at the end of March. The shipment of medical supplies <a href="https://www.natoaktual.cz/zpravy/spanelsko-italie-pomoc-ochranny-oblek-koronavirus-covid-armada-nato-aliance.A200330_112740_na_zpravy_m00">was transported by</a> a Spanish military C-130 Hercules aircraft.</p><p> The Czech Republic also <a href="https://apps.odok.cz/attachment/-/down/RCIABNNLWCXP">provided</a> (.pdf) one million protective masks to North Macedonia in April and also <a href="https://www.mzv.cz/ljubljana/cz/cr_a_evropska_unie/ceska_republika_zapujcila_slovinsku.html">showed</a> solidarity with Slovenia, to which it lent 500,000 masks, 25,000 respirators and 5,000 protective suits at the end of March. In April, the Czech Republic then supplied Slovenia with another one million surgical masks and another 200,000 respirators. Last but not least, the Czech Republic also <a href="https://www.mvcr.cz/clanek/ceska-republika-pomuze-v-boji-s-koronavirem-take-san-marinu.aspx">provided</a> part of its air bridge capacity for the transport of medical supplies from other countries, for example <a href="https://www.novinky.cz/koronavirus/clanek/madarsko-a-slovensko-obdrzely-zasilky-zdravotniho-materialu-z-ciny-40317892">to Slovakia</a> .</p>
