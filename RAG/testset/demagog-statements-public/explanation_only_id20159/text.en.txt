<p>At its meeting on January 4, 2021, the government <a href="https://www.vlada.cz/cz/media-centrum/aktualne/vlada-schvalila-dalsi-pomoc-pro-uzavrene-provozovny--prodlouzit-chce-i-snazsi-administraci-socialnich-davek-behem-pandemie-185909/">approved</a> a new program to support entrepreneurs affected by government measures to limit the spread of the covid-19 disease. The COVID - Gastro - Closed Establishments subsidy program <a href="https://www.mpo.cz/cz/rozcestnik/informace-o-koronavirus/program-covid-_-gastro-_-uzavrene-provozovny--258742/">applies to</a> those entrepreneurs (natural or legal persons) whose business activities were restricted from October 14, 2020 to January 10, 2021, which caused a decrease in their sales. These are mainly restaurants, retail stores or services. Entrepreneurs will have CZK <a href="https://www.vlada.cz/cz/media-centrum/aktualne/vlada-schvalila-dalsi-pomoc-pro-uzavrene-provozovny--prodlouzit-chce-i-snazsi-administraci-socialnich-davek-behem-pandemie-185909/">2.5 billion</a> available under this call.</p><p> The financial support will <a href="https://www.mpo.cz/cz/rozcestnik/informace-o-koronavirus/program-covid-_-gastro-_-uzavrene-provozovny--258742/">amount to</a> CZK 400 per employee (including cooperating self-employed persons) for the days when the restrictive regulation was in force for the relevant business area. The support will be available for business operations and costs, including depreciation, taxes and loan repayments. Applications will be accepted from January 15, 2021.</p>
