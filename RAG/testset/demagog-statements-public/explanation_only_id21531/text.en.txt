<p>The former government <a href="https://www.vlada.cz/cz/media-centrum/aktualne/vlada-schvalila-podpurne-programy-pro-podnikatele-postizene-protiepidemickymi-opatrenimi-192462/">introduced</a> several programs to help employees, businesses, and the self-employed (SVČ) during the Covid-19 pandemic.</p><p> As for the current <a href="https://covid.gov.cz/situace/kompenzace">compensation</a> programs, the Ministry of Labor and Social Affairs <a href="https://www.mpo.cz/cz/rozcestnik/informace-o-koronavirus/covid-dotacni-rozcestnik--259263/">includes</a> , for example, <a href="https://covid.gov.cz/situace/kompenzace/antivirus-nucene-omezeni-provozu-karanteny">the Antivirus A</a> and <a href="https://covid.gov.cz/situace/kompenzace/antivirus-b">Antivirus B</a> programs. The purpose of the first of these is <a href="https://www.vlada.cz/cz/epidemie-koronaviru/dulezite-informace/podpora-a-ulevy-pro-podnikatele-a-zamestnance-180601/">to compensate</a> employers whose employees were ordered to quarantine or isolate. Antivirus B was to <a href="https://www.vlada.cz/cz/epidemie-koronaviru/dulezite-informace/podpora-a-ulevy-pro-podnikatele-a-zamestnance-180601/">compensate</a> companies if they had to limit operations due to a significant number of employees in quarantine, or, for example, if demand for their services or products is limited due to the pandemic.</p><p> For injured self-employed persons, but also for partners of small companies and people working on the DPČ and DPP, there is a <a href="https://www.mfcr.cz/cs/aktualne/tiskove-zpravy/2021/od-22-listopadu-bude-pokracovat-kompenza-43629">Compensation Bonus</a> program, which <a href="https://www.mpo.cz/cz/rozcestnik/informace-o-koronavirus/covid-dotacni-rozcestnik--259263/">falls</a> under the Ministry of Finance.</p><p> For <a href="https://www.mpo.cz/covid-2021">example,</a> the Ministry of Industry and Trade was supposed to be responsible for <a href="https://covid.gov.cz/situace/kompenzace/covid-2021">the COVID 2021</a> subsidy program and the <a href="https://covid.gov.cz/situace/kompenzace/covid-nepokryte-naklady">COVID Uncovered Costs</a> program, <a href="https://www.mpo.cz/cz/rozcestnik/pro-media/tiskove-zpravy/nova-podoba-kompenzaci-k-programu-na-nepokryte-naklady-vlada-schvalila-take-program-covid---2021-s-podporou-500-kc-za-den-a-zamestnance---259983/">intended</a> for companies with a significant drop in sales. Although they were already prepared, the government ultimately decided <a href="https://www.podnikatel.cz/aktuality/firmy-maji-smulu-novy-ministr-odmitl-programy-covid-2021-a-nepokryte-naklady/">not to</a> relaunch them.</p><p> Let us add that there are currently some programs whose purpose is primarily to provide entrepreneurs with loan <a href="https://covid.gov.cz/situace/kompenzace">guarantees</a> , but these are not classic compensations. We can mention, for example, the <a href="https://covid.gov.cz/situace/kompenzace/zaruka-covid-sport">COVID Sport</a> guarantee program under the Ministry of Industry and Trade. It also managed the recently completed <a href="https://covid.gov.cz/situace/kompenzace/covid-zaruka-ck-prijem-zadosti-ukoncen">COVID Travel Agency</a> Guarantee program for travel agencies, which was followed by <a href="https://www.nrb.cz/zaruky-cestovnim-kancelarim-bude-nrb-poskytovat-i-v-roce-2022/">the Travel Agency Guarantee</a> program of the Ministry for Regional Development.</p><p> The above information shows that compensation programs are indeed divided primarily between the three mentioned ministries. We therefore assess Marian Jurečka&#39;s statement as true.</p>
