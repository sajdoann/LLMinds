<p>In June 2023, the government of Petr Fiala adopted <a href="https://odok.cz/portal/zvlady/usneseni/2023/449/">a resolution</a> ( <a href="https://odok.cz/portal/services/download/attachment/2023/449/pdf/">.pdf</a> ) approving the preliminary proposal of the Ministry of Finance on the amount of state budget revenues and expenditures for 2024. This was not a finished government budget proposal, but only a <a href="https://ct24.ceskatelevize.cz/domaci/3595254-vlada-schvalila-predbezny-navrh-rozpoctu-na-rok-2024-se-schodkem-235-miliard">preliminary</a> plan that did not contain a detailed outline of individual components on the revenue and expenditure side. This proposal <a href="https://www.mfcr.cz/cs/ministerstvo/media/tiskove-zpravy/2023/vlada-schvalila-predpokladane-parametry-51706">calculated</a> a deficit of CZK 235 billion. Prime Minister Petr Fiala <a href="https://www.irozhlas.cz/ekonomika/statni-rozpocet-schodek-vlada-stanjura-fiala_2306212154_har">added</a> to the proposal that the final budget deficit would not exceed CZK 270 billion, which the government had already set as the budget deficit <a href="https://ct24.ceskatelevize.cz/domaci/3594452-je-jen-ramec-rekl-o-270miliardovem-deficitu-kupka-podle-havlicka-je-vlada-chaoticka">ceiling</a> <a href="https://www.irozhlas.cz/ekonomika/vlada-schodek-snizit-na-270-miliard-korun_2306142120_hof">before</a> adopting the resolution.</p><p> At the end <a href="https://www.odok.cz/portal/veklep/material/KORNCV8KBV8T/">of August</a> , the Ministry of Finance <a href="https://www.mfcr.cz/cs/ministerstvo/media/tiskove-zpravy/2023/navrh-rozpoctu-na-rok-2024-52808">submitted</a> to the government the final draft budget for 2024, which envisages total revenues of CZK 1,921.2 billion and expenditures of CZK 2,173.2 billion. The budget deficit is thus <a href="https://ct24.ceskatelevize.cz/ekonomika/3611504-ministerstvo-predlozilo-vlade-navrh-rozpoctu-se-schodkem-252-miliard">planned</a> at CZK 252 billion ( <a href="https://1drv.ms/w/s!AuXRjwyZOj5-szj-hbJ8QyXVm69H?e=n9R2tm">.docx</a> , page 1).</p><p> To summarize, the Ministry of Finance proposed a state budget deficit of CZK 235 billion for next year. In fact, this was not a government proposal, but only a preliminary plan for the ministry. Even before the approval of this proposal, cabinet representatives stated that the deficit would not exceed CZK 270 billion. The final budget proposal for 2024 then calculates a deficit of CZK 252 billion. We therefore assess the statement as true.</p>
