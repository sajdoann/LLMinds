<p>Prime Minister Petr Fiala responds to the question of whether he plans to visit the regions in a similar way to what Interior Minister Vít Rakušan is currently doing with his <i>&quot;No Censorship&quot;</i> <a href="https://zpravy.aktualne.cz/domaci/rakusan-karvina/r~8775e82eb9bf11eebc030cc47ab5f122/">discussions</a> or <a href="https://karvinsky.denik.cz/zpravy_region/babis-tour-karvinsko-tesin-havirov-napadali-priznivci-odpurci2022.html">Andrej Babiš</a> has done in the past. The Prime Minister states that he regularly visits smaller towns and villages and cites as an example a visit to a school and an agricultural enterprise during a trade union strike in Prague.</p><p> The union strike <a href="https://www.irozhlas.cz/zpravy-domov/stavka-online-aktualne-27-11-listopadu-pondeli-2023_2311270600_ako">took place</a> on November 27, 2023 and was also reported as a school and teacher <a href="https://www.seznamzpravy.cz/tag/stavka-ucitelu-99052">strike</a> . In total, 74% of kindergartens, primary and secondary schools <a href="https://www.novinky.cz/clanek/domaci-on-line-den-hnevu-zacina-40452023">participated</a> in the strike, with <a href="https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/viewer?mid=10XvG0USrtOrpjn2FTRB21tml3wV54Fc&amp;femb=1&amp;ll=50.0988857318867%2C14.352953236445156&amp;z=11">some</a> of them remaining completely closed. In addition to the school unions, the civil service, timber and healthcare unions also <a href="https://ct24.ceskatelevize.cz/clanek/domaci/odbory-usporadaly-stavku-proti-vladnim-usporam-v-nekterych-firmach-se-prace-zastavila-jinde-se-lide-343560">declared</a> a strike, and some companies also suspended operations. The strike was mainly aimed <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20231204205016/https:/www.ceskenoviny.cz/zpravy/2442475">at pushing for</a> a higher budget for education, while some unions <a href="https://ct24.ceskatelevize.cz/clanek/domaci/jsme-pripraveni-pokracovat-v-protestech-rekl-stredula-na-odborarske-demonstraci-343569">protested</a> against the government&#39;s consolidation package. On the other hand, the Teachers&#39; Platform, for example, <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20231204184939/https:/www.ceskenoviny.cz/zpravy/2446104">distanced itself</a> from the union protest.</p><p> On the day of the strike, Petr Fiala <a href="https://www.idnes.cz/jihlava/zpravy/premier-fiala-ministr-zemedelstvi-vyborny-navsteva-skola-pozdatin.A231127_063333_jihlava-zpravy_mv">visited</a> <a href="https://www.agroskola.eu/">the Agro school</a> in Pozątín in the Třebíč region, where he arrived together with the Minister of Agriculture Marek Výborný (KDU-ČSL). At the school, they also <a href="https://www.facebook.com/photo?fbid=909295700559834&amp;set=pb.100044382371042.-2207520000">met</a> ( <a href="https://www.idnes.cz/jihlava/zpravy/premier-fiala-ministr-zemedelstvi-vyborny-navsteva-skola-pozdatin.A231127_063333_jihlava-zpravy_mv/foto/V231127_122830_idnestv_jda">video</a> ) with teachers and students, with whom they debated. They <a href="https://www.idnes.cz/jihlava/zpravy/premier-fiala-ministr-zemedelstvi-vyborny-navsteva-skola-pozdatin.A231127_063333_jihlava-zpravy_mv#:~:text=Z%20Poz%C4%8Fat%C3%ADna%20premi%C3%A9r%20spole%C4%8Dn%C4%9B%20s%20V%C3%BDborn%C3%BDm%20zam%C3%AD%C5%99ili%20do%20Jihomoravsk%C3%A9ho%20kraje%2C%20konkr%C3%A9tn%C4%9B%20do%20Nov%C3%A9%20Vsi%20nedaleko%20Ivan%C4%8Dic%2C%20kde%20je%20v%20pl%C3%A1nu%20setk%C3%A1n%C3%AD%20se%20zem%C4%9Bd%C4%9Blci%20ze%20zem%C4%9Bd%C4%9Blsk%C3%A9ho%20dru%C5%BEstva%20Pooslav%C3%AD.%20Sv%C5%AFj%20pond%C4%9Bln%C3%AD%20program%20zah%C3%A1jil%20r%C3%A1no%20v%20Brn%C4%9B%2C%20kde%20se%20setkal%20v%20Hotelu%20International%20s%20novin%C3%A1%C5%99i.">then</a> <a href="https://www.idnes.cz/zpravy/domaci/premier-petr-fiala-ministr-zemedelstvi-cesko-protest-navsteva.A231127_122709_domaci_vank">visited</a> a meeting with farmers in the Pooslaví cooperative in Nová Ves. Lukáš Jurečka, brother of the Minister of Labor and Social Affairs Marian Jurečka, is <a href="https://archive.ph/zcMIp">the director</a> of this cooperative. Previously, he was actively <a href="https://or.justice.cz/ias/ui/rejstrik-firma.vysledky?subjektId=589387&amp;typ=UPLNY">involved</a> in the management of the cooperative as a member of the board of directors and later as its vice-chairman, serving in these positions from February 2, 2010 to June 28, 2021. </p><figure class="image"><img src="https://1884403144.rsc.cdn77.org/foto/fiala-jurecka/Zml0LWluLzk3OHg5OTk5L2ZpbHRlcnM6cXVhbGl0eSg4NSk6bm9fdXBzY2FsZSgpOmZvY2FsKDMwNHgzMDE6MTQ0M3g0NTIpL2ltZw/8771630.jpg?v=0&amp;st=oTOnIrrHiV-OlAasMasvXonM9hhCbVrQfhDZIwmBk6E&amp;ts=1600812000&amp;e=0"><figcaption> Source: <a href="https://www.blesk.cz/clanek/zpravy-politika/760473/odborari-protestovali-v-praze-premier-jel-za-druzstevniky-proc-byl-fiala-u-jureckova-bratra.html">Blesk.cz</a></figcaption></figure><p> During <a href="https://vlada.gov.cz/cz/clenove-vlady/historie-minulych-vlad/prehled-vlad-cr/1993-2013-cr/bohuslav_sobotka/prehled-clenu-vlady-bohuslava-sobotky-29-01-2014---trva-124534/">the government</a> of Bohuslav Sobotka, when Marian Jurečka was <a href="https://vlada.gov.cz/cz/clenove-vlady/marian-jurecka-191697/#:~:text=2014%E2%80%932017%3A%20ministr%20zem%C4%9Bd%C4%9Blstv%C3%AD%20%C4%8CR">the Minister</a> of Agriculture, Lukáš Jurečka worked as his <a href="https://www.idnes.cz/zpravy/domaci/ministerstvo-zemedelstvi-marian-jurecka-bratr-lukas-jurecka-poradce-kdu-csl.A170918_145202_domaci_fer">advisor</a> . In the past, for example, a half-million <a href="https://neovlivni.cz/dotacni-parazit-miliony-z-verejnych-penez-pro-ministrova-bratra/">subsidy</a> for the cooperative for the demonstration farm <a href="https://agronavigator.cz/demo-objekty/demonstracni-farmy">project</a> , which was a new subsidy project <a href="https://www.kdu.cz/aktualne/archiv/2017/ministerstvo-zemedelstvi-spusti-dotacni-program-pr">introduced</a> under Marian Jurečka, was also laundered. Petr Fiala, in an interview verified by us (similarly to <a href="https://www.blesk.cz/clanek/zpravy-politika/760375/fiala-v-blesku-zvysit-nektere-dane-bylo-nutne-odsoudil-vulgarity-pri-stavce-pavlovi-ustoupil-s-projevem.html#:~:text=%E2%80%9ETo%20nen%C3%AD%20%C5%BE%C3%A1dn%C3%A1%20farma%2C%20kter%C3%A1%20pat%C5%99%C3%AD%20panu%20Jure%C4%8Dkovi%2C%E2%80%9C%20dodal%20Fiala%20s%20t%C3%ADm%2C%20%C5%BEe%20Luk%C3%A1%C5%A1%20Jure%C4%8Dka%20tam%20d%C4%9Bl%C3%A1%20jednoho%20z%20vedouc%C3%ADch%20a%20s%C3%A1m%20Fiala%20se%20to%20podle%20sv%C3%BDch%20slov%20dozv%C4%9Bd%C4%9Bl%20a%C5%BE%20na%20posledn%C3%AD%20chv%C3%ADli.%C2%A0">before</a> ), stated that he did not know that Minister Jurečka&#39;s brother worked in the cooperative and only found out about it on the spot ( <a href="https://cnn.iprima.cz/fiala-babise-musime-porazit-i-v-pristich-volbach-spolehame-na-politicka-reseni-ne-soudy-425484">video</a> , time 21:24).</p><p> It should be added that Prime Minister Fiala was <a href="https://www.idnes.cz/zpravy/domaci/premier-petr-fiala-navsteva-druzstva-protesty-zmena-programu.A231127_133155_domaci_vank">criticized</a> for visiting Vysočina during a trade union strike. According to Jakub Tomek from the Prime Minister&#39;s Communications Department, <a href="https://www.idnes.cz/jihlava/zpravy/premier-fiala-ministr-zemedelstvi-vyborny-navsteva-skola-pozdatin.A231127_063333_jihlava-zpravy_mv">it was</a> a regular working visit that was in no way related to the strike in the education sector. The Agro School <a href="https://www.idnes.cz/jihlava/zpravy/premier-fiala-ministr-zemedelstvi-vyborny-navsteva-skola-pozdatin.A231127_063333_jihlava-zpravy_mv">did not join</a> the protests, and normal classes were held there.</p><p> <strong>Summary</strong></p><p> On the day of the trade union strike, which took place on November 27, 2023, Petr Fiala first visited the Agro School in Pozątín in the Třebíč region, where he discussed with students and teachers. He then moved to the Pooslaví cooperative in Nová Ves. Minister Jurečka&#39;s brother, Lukáš Jurečka, is the director of the cooperative and in the past served as a member of the board of directors and later as vice-chairman of the board of directors. We therefore assess the Prime Minister&#39;s statement as true.</p>
