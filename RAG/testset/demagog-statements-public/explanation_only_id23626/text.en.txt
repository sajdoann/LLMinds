<p>In an interview, Vít Rakušan (STAN) responds to a question about <a href="https://www.irozhlas.cz/zpravy-domov/platy-politiku-soudcu-zvyseni-jurecka-navrh-schillerova_2410131347_har">the proposal of</a> the Minister of Labor and Social Affairs Marian Jurečka to increase the salaries of deputies and other constitutional officials. He mentions that politicians should not decide on their salaries. In this context, he adds that, in his opinion, the agreement concluded around 2015 by the then government <a href="https://vlada.gov.cz/cz/media-centrum/dulezite-dokumenty/koalicni-smlouva-mezi-cssd--hnutim-ano-2011-a-kdu-csl-na-volebni-obdobi-2013---2017-115459/">coalition</a> and the opposition, the aim of which was to automate the growth of politicians&#39; salaries, should have been adhered to. According to Rakušan, this agreement was to ensure that salaries would increase in the event of a good economic situation, and vice versa, they would not increase if the economy were to decline.</p><p> The Minister of the Interior is talking about the time <a href="https://vlada.gov.cz/cz/media-centrum/dulezite-dokumenty/koalicni-smlouva-mezi-cssd--hnutim-ano-2011-a-kdu-csl-na-volebni-obdobi-2013---2017-115459/">of the government of</a> Bohuslav Sobotka, when the government coalition consisted of the Social Democrats, ANO and the People&#39;s Party. The Mayors and Independents (STAN) and the <a href="https://demagog.cz/vyrok/21953#:~:text=zasedl%20v%C2%A0m%C3%ADstop%C5%99edsednictvu.-,Starostov%C3%A9%20pro%20Libereck%C3%BD%20kraj,-Specifickou%20roli%20m%C3%A1">closely</a> linked Mayors for <a href="https://www.starostove-nezavisli.cz/regiony/liberecky-kraj">the Liberec Region</a> (SLK) were <a href="https://www.psp.cz/sqw/organy2.sqw?k=1&amp;o=7">part of</a> the opposition at that time. In the 2013 elections to the Chamber of Deputies, they specifically <a href="https://www.top09.cz/co-delame/medialni-vystupy/spoluprace-mezi-top-09-a-stan-byla-uspesna-21659.html">ran</a> on the TOP 09 candidate list and <a href="https://www.volby.cz/pls/ps2013/ps111?xjazyk=CZ&amp;xkraj=0&amp;xstrana=0&amp;xv=2&amp;xt=2">won</a> a total of four mandates. After one of the STAN deputies became <a href="https://www.irozhlas.cz/node/5932559">an MEP</a> in <a href="https://www.top09.cz/regiony/praha/regionalni-organizace/praha-4/top-09-ziskala-pro-prahu-4-poslanecky-mandat-16388.html">2014</a> , <a href="https://www.volby.cz/pls/ps2013/ps111?xjazyk=CZ&amp;xkraj=0&amp;xstrana=0&amp;xv=2&amp;xt=2">the Mayors</a> and SLK were represented in the Chamber only by Věra Kovářová, Petr Gazdík and Jan Farský.</p><p> <strong>Old-new vending machine</strong></p><p> The salaries of members of parliament and other constitutional officials are regulated by <a href="https://www.zakonyprolidi.cz/cs/1995-236">the Act</a> on the Salaries of State Officials. <a href="https://www.zakonyprolidi.cz/cs/2002-309#f2337709">The automatic</a> mechanism for increasing these salaries became part of the Act in <a href="https://www.zakonyprolidi.cz/cs/2002-309#f2337901">January</a> <a href="https://www.e-sbirka.cz/sb/1995/236/2004-01-01">2004.</a> Its version at that time <a href="https://vlada.gov.cz/cz/media-centrum/aktualne/mpsv-pocita-platy-ustavnich-cinitelu-podle-platneho-zakona-126166/">linked</a> the salary base, from which salaries are <a href="https://www.e-sbirka.cz/sb/1995/236/2004-01-01#par_8-odst_1">further</a> <a href="https://www.zakonyprolidi.cz/cs/1995-236#p8">calculated</a> , to the average wage in <a href="https://statistikaamy.csu.gov.cz/csu-nebude-publikovat-data-za-mzdy-v-nepodnikatelske-sfere#:~:text=Nepodnikatelsk%C3%A1%20sf%C3%A9ra%20potom,n%C4%9Bkter%C3%A9%20p%C5%99%C3%ADsp%C4%9Bvkov%C3%A9%20organizace.">the non-business</a> <a href="https://csu.gov.cz/informace-na-zaklade-zvlastnich-zakonu">sector</a> for the calendar year before last. At that time, salaries were not yet linked to the average wage in the entire <a href="https://www.idnes.cz/zpravy/domaci/snemovna-zmrazeni-platu-platu-politiku-navazani-na-prumernou-mzdu.A201112_062129_domaci_kop">national</a> <a href="https://csu.gov.cz/1-pmz_m">economy</a> .</p><p> The law therefore contained the automatic mechanism already before 2015, which Vít Rakušan is talking about, but <a href="https://www.seznamzpravy.cz/clanek/domaci-politika-prehledne-jak-rostly-platy-politiku-ve-srovnani-s-duchody-a-mzdami-227935">it was not</a> used in some years because Parliament decided to freeze salaries. The salary base of politicians was not calculated automatically, for example, in the years <a href="https://www.zakonyprolidi.cz/cs/2003-427#p3">2004</a> , <a href="https://www.zakonyprolidi.cz/cs/2007-261#f3811744">2008</a> to 2010, and salaries were later frozen in the years <a href="https://www.e-sbirka.cz/sb/1995/236/2011-01-01#par_3a">2011</a> to <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20140330092920/https:/www.zakonyprolidi.cz/cs/1995-236#p3a">2014</a> .</p><p> The last mentioned <a href="https://www.zakonyprolidi.cz/cs/2010-425">freeze</a> for the years 2011–2014 was enforced during the economic crisis by the former <a href="https://vlada.gov.cz/cz/clenove-vlady/historie-minulych-vlad/prehled-vlad-cr/1993-2010-cr/petr-necas/prehled-byvalych-clenu-vlady-petra-necase-96390/">Nečas government</a> ( <a href="https://www.psp.cz/sqw/text/orig2.sqw?idd=64298&amp;pdf=1">.pdf</a> , pp. 2, 9) and was <a href="https://ct24.ceskatelevize.cz/clanek/domaci/dohady-konci-platy-politiku-vzrostou-o-necela-tri-procenta-341349">to end</a> in January 2015. If legislators had not approved another amendment to the law by then, the salaries of constitutional officials would have <a href="https://ct24.ceskatelevize.cz/clanek/domaci/dohady-konci-platy-politiku-vzrostou-o-necela-tri-procenta-341349">increased</a> by 26% at the beginning of 2015. In the second half of 2014, i.e. during <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20241007185901/https:/vlada.gov.cz/cz/clenove-vlady/historie-minulych-vlad/prehled-vlad-cr/1993-2013-cr/bohuslav_sobotka/prehled-clenu-vlady-bohuslava-sobotky-29-01-2014---trva-124534/">the Sobotka</a> cabinet, the Chamber of Deputies was therefore <a href="https://ct24.ceskatelevize.cz/clanek/domaci/o-kolik-si-maji-poslanci-zvysit-platy-uz-se-neshodne-ani-cssd-340604">discussing</a> a draft <a href="https://www.psp.cz/sqw/historie.sqw?o=7&amp;t=277">amendment</a> that was to resolve the calculation of salaries in the following year.</p><p> Finally, <a href="https://www.zakonyprolidi.cz/cs/2014-359">an amendment</a> was passed <a href="https://www.psp.cz/sqw/historie.sqw?o=7&amp;t=277">by the parliament</a> , which again linked politicians&#39; salaries to the average wage in the non-business sector, and thus, after more than five years, once again introduced an automatic mechanism for calculating salaries. Unlike <a href="https://www.e-sbirka.cz/sb/1995/236/2013-01-01#par_3-odst_3">the previous</a> version, this old-new mechanism determined two <a href="https://www.zakonyprolidi.cz/cs/2014-359">different</a> salary bases, one for judges and the other for other constitutional officials, i.e. deputies, senators, etc. Above all, it stipulated that instead of the previous <a href="https://www.e-sbirka.cz/sb/1995/236/2013-01-01#par_3-odst_3">2.75 times</a> the average wage (in the non-business sector), the salary base of politicians would correspond to <a href="https://www.zakonyprolidi.cz/cs/2014-359">2.25 times</a> the average wage from 2015.</p><p> For the following three years, the amendment <a href="https://www.zakonyprolidi.cz/cs/2014-359">set</a> a gradual increase in the salary base, which in 2018 corresponded to 2.5 times the average wage. For the following years, <a href="https://www.zakonyprolidi.cz/cs/2014-359">the amendment</a> did not include further adjustments, which meant that from 2019 the base would return to <a href="https://www.e-sbirka.cz/sb/1995/236/2014-10-01#par_3-odst_3">2.75 times</a> the average wage. (This was still the average wage in the non-business sector, not directly in <a href="https://www.idnes.cz/zpravy/domaci/snemovna-zmrazeni-platu-platu-politiku-navazani-na-prumernou-mzdu.A201112_062129_domaci_kop">the national economy</a> . The salary base is only <a href="https://www.zakonyprolidi.cz/cs/2020-587#f6962191">derived</a> from it thanks to the proposal ( <a href="https://www.psp.cz/sqw/text/orig2.sqw?idd=77982&amp;pdf=1">.pdf</a> ) <a href="https://www.idnes.cz/zpravy/domaci/snemovna-zmrazeni-platu-platu-politiku-navazani-na-prumernou-mzdu.A201112_062129_domaci_kop">of SOCDEM</a> , ANO and Pirates <a href="https://www.psp.cz/eknih/2017ps/stenprot/066schuz/s066004.htm#h6">promoted</a> in 2020.)</p><p> <strong>Agreement and role of STAN</strong></p><p> All three members of the Mayors&#39; Council <a href="https://www.psp.cz/sqw/hlasy.sqw?G=60089">abstained</a> from voting on the 2014 <a href="https://www.psp.cz/sqw/historie.sqw?o=7&amp;t=277">amendment</a> . The same was true <a href="https://www.psp.cz/sqw/hlasy.sqw?G=60088">of</a> the vote on the <a href="https://www.psp.cz/eknih/2013ps/stenprot/023schuz/s023133.htm#h40">amendment by</a> Jan Hamáček (SOCDEM), which transformed the original draft of the amendment into its final form ( <a href="https://www.psp.cz/sqw/text/orig2.sqw?idd=112107">.pdf</a> , pp. 3–4). The initial version of the amendment only envisaged a simple freezing of the base at approximately CZK 52,000 ( <a href="https://www.psp.cz/sqw/text/orig2.sqw?idd=110647">.pdf</a> , pp. 3 of 8).</p><p> There were other amendments at play at the time. The main one was the proposal of the parliamentary budget committee, which also demanded the re-introduction of the automatic system. It advocated a faster increase in salaries than was ultimately approved ( <a href="https://www.psp.cz/sqw/text/orig2.sqw?idd=112107">.pdf</a> , pp. 1–2). For example, for 2015 it already envisaged a salary base reaching 2.5 times the average wage. This would ensure higher salaries for MPs and senators starting in 2015.</p><p> In November 2014, when the amendment was being discussed by the Chamber of Deputies, some media <a href="https://zpravy.aktualne.cz/domaci/politika/snemovni-vybor-doporucil-zvysit-poslanecky-plat-o-8100-korun/r~9f18dfac70b011e4840b002590604f2e/">reported</a> that <i>the “deputy clubs”</i> had agreed on the budget committee’s amendment proposal. At the same time, news servers quoted the then chairman of the joint TOP 09 and Starosty parliamentary group, <a href="https://www.psp.cz/sqw/detail.sqw?id=377&amp;o=7">Miroslav Kalousek</a> , as <a href="https://zpravy.aktualne.cz/domaci/politika/snemovni-vybor-doporucil-zvysit-poslanecky-plat-o-8100-korun/r~9f18dfac70b011e4840b002590604f2e/">stating</a> that the government coalition <i>“made an agreement”</i> and TOP 09 and Starosty <i>“joined”</i> it. <a href="https://public.psp.cz/eknih/2013ps/stenprot/023schuz/s023010.htm">Later,</a> before the final vote on the amendment, Kalousek <a href="https://public.psp.cz/eknih/2013ps/stenprot/023schuz/s023011.htm">complained</a> that SOCDEM had violated this agreement by submitting Hamáček’s proposal.</p><p> Marian Jurečka from the then coalition KDU-ČSL also <a href="https://ct24.ceskatelevize.cz/clanek/domaci/babis-k-boji-o-platy-poslancu-sobotka-je-populista-podrazil-koalici-341692">spoke</a> in a similar way to Kalousek in December 2014. According to him, the budget committee&#39;s proposal was first agreed upon by the coalition among itself and <i>&quot;eventually (...) also in the Chamber of Deputies with all the clubs&quot;.</i> The then chairman of the budget committee <a href="https://web.archive.org/web/20150318090423/http:/zpravy.e15.cz/domaci/politika/spicky-cssd-jsou-proti-zvyseni-platu-politiku-o-14-procent-1139067">Václav Votava</a> (SOCDEM), however, mentioned that the amendment proposal in the committee <a href="https://www.echo24.cz/a/iQz2V/sobotka-dal-sobotka-vzal-podivna-hra-o-poslanecke-platy">was created</a> on the basis of an agreement that SOCDEM MP Roman Sklenák negotiated <i>&quot;with the other club chairmen&quot;</i> .</p><p> It is not possible to verify from the available sources whether or not STAN members of parliament were directly involved in the original negotiations on the form of the automatic mechanism. <a href="https://www.psp.cz/sqw/hlasy.sqw?G=60089">They neither supported</a> nor voted against the resulting amendment that reintroduced the automatic mechanism during the subsequent vote. It is not clear whether they disagreed with the introduction of the automatic mechanism as such, or with its parameters, which were contained in Jan Hamáček&#39;s successful proposal.</p><p> <strong>Conclusion</strong></p><p> Although the media published some information about how negotiations on an automatic mechanism for increasing politicians&#39; salaries took place at the turn of 2014 and 2015, based on available sources, it is neither possible to confirm nor deny whether representatives of the STAN movement participated in these agreements. For this reason, we assess Vít Rakušan&#39;s statement as unverifiable.</p>
