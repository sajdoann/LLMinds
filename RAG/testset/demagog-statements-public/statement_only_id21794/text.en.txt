Václav Havel granted two thousand pardons, Václav Klaus four hundred, I twenty, or more precisely twenty-one. Among these pardons there was no parricide, because Václav Havel granted a pardon to the parricide Mr. Odložil, there was no restitution fraudster, while Václav Havel granted a pardon to Mrs. Chadimová, who was then defended by Pavel Rychetský.
